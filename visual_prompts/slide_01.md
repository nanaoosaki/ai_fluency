# Slide 01 Generation Prompt: Beyond Prompting

## Purpose

Opening cover slide for a ten-minute talk on documentation-driven AI collaboration.

## Exact Text To Render

Render exactly this text, with correct spelling and punctuation, and no additional words:

```text
AI FLUENCY / CONTEXT SYSTEMS

Beyond
Prompting

Building a context system that lets AI work with you

CORE THESIS
A model gives capability.
A maintained context system makes it iterative, reviewable, and reusable.
```

## Visual Direction

Create a refined 16:9 editorial keynote cover. Warm paper background, graphite typography, one muted forest-green emphasis on the word `Prompting`, and a quiet documentation-workbench atmosphere: faint blank paper edges and an organized thread or annotation trace in the negative space. The typography is the dominant visual object.

## Layout

- Small uppercase kicker at upper left.
- Monumental two-line title aligned left in the upper-middle area.
- Subtitle beneath title.
- Thesis sentence anchored near the lower left across one or two lines.
- Keep a large field of quiet negative space.

## Requirements

- Full final presentation slide, not a background.
- Accurate, highly legible English typography suitable for projection.
- No logos, watermarks, fake UI, extra labels, or illegible pseudo-text.
